Privacy Policy for Kural Thalam

This Privacy Policy explains how the "Kural Thalam" mobile application ("the App") handles your information.

By using the App, you agree to this policy.

1. Information Handling

  • No Personal Data Collection: The Kural Thalam App is designed to provide you with access to Thirukkural verses and details based on your numerical input. We do not collect any personal identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, phone number, location, or any other data that could identify you personally.
  • Offline Functionality & Local Data: All Thirukkural details are stored within the App's asset files (JSON) on your device. The App functions entirely offline and does not transmit any user input or data to any external server. Your interactions with the App, including numbers you type, remain private on your device.
  • No Usage Tracking/Analytics: We do not use any analytics tools or tracking mechanisms to monitor your usage of the App.

2. How We Use Data

Since no personal data is collected, we do not use your information for any purpose other than providing the core functionality of displaying Thirukkural details on your device.

3. Data Storage & Security

All data (Thirukkural content) is stored locally within the App on your device. No data is transmitted to or stored on our servers.

4. Data Sharing

As we do not collect any personal data or usage information, we do not share any data with third parties.

5. Your Controls

You can stop all data handling by the App simply by uninstalling it from your device.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is designed for a general audience and does not collect any personal information from children.

7. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy if the App's functionality changes in the future (e.g., if we were to introduce online features). We will post any changes on this page.
